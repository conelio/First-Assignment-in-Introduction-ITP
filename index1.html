Project Idea

My project idea is to develop a centralized clinical medical software for primary care physicians to enter patient clinical information and demographics and to be accessible by all authorized personal in Australia. This software will be called Universal Medical Clinical Software. Currently there are about five clinical medical softwares dominated by two softwares: Medical Director and Best Practice The other three are minor players. As of writting the current softwares do not have compulsory demographic captures such as capturing blood pressure, height and weight of patients. A patient can be seen by a primary physcian for years without blood their blood pressure being checked and not recorded.In addition identification of Indigenious people and other minority groups is not happening making it impossible to close the health gap between main stream and these disadvantaged groups. The current clinical data is not coherent and diagnosis are not properly coded making it difficult to collect data and apply preventative health and clinicians cannot share as the systems do not communicate with each and information is not being fed into the central My Health Records created by the federal government. My program will capture clinical and demorgraphic data and will be live across Australia and accessible anywhere by authorized clinicians.
